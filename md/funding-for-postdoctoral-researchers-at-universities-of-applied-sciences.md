# Funding for postdoctoral researchers at universities of applied sciences

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/funding-for-postdoctoral-researchers-at-universities-of-applied-sciences

The grant offers PhD-qualified lecturers and researchers at universities of applied sciences a chance to further develop their research careers, linking their position as a PhD-qualified researcher with the roles of change agent, innovator, and professional. For universities of applied sciences, a postdoctoral position offers the opportunity to strengthen the research capacity and the connection between education and research.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Open for applications  
**Closing date full application:** 9 June 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €3,000,000  
**Research programme:** Hbo-postdoc 2025-2030
