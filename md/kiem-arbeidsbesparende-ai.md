# KIEM Arbeidsbesparende AI

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kiem-arbeidsbesparende-ai

The aim of KIEM Arbeidsbesparende AI is to conduct (explorative) applied research through new practice-oriented research activities focused on the application of testable and existing AI applications (use cases) in organizations (SMEs and/or public organizations) based on questions and needs from professional practice. This AI application thus contributes to labor savings and/or productivity increases within the organization of the partner(s) where and with whom the research is carried out.
