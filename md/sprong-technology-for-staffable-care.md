# SPRONG Technology for Staffable Care

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/sprong-technology-for-staffable-care

With SPRONG Technology for Staffable Care, the Taskforce for Applied Research SIA wants to bring together and strengthen applied research, acquired knowledge and experience related to the application of technology in healthcare in a multidisciplinary SPRONG research group. A SPRONG group has the potential to develop into a powerful research group within an eight year period and consists of a collaboration between (at least) two universities of applied sciences and practice partners, such as companies from professional practice, public institutions and governments.
