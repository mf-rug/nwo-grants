# Financing Professional Doctorate-candidates 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/financing-professional-doctorate-candidates-2026

Universities of applied sciences can apply for funding for PD candidates. A maximum of € 271,400 can be requested per application, of which a maximum of € 258,000 is intended for an allowance for labour costs and a maximum of € 13,400 for travel and study costs.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Continuous  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €16,284,000  
**Research programme:** Financiering PD-kandidaten 2023-2027
