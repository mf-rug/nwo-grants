# Doctoral Grant for Teachers, 2nd round 2025

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/doctoral-grant-for-teachers-2nd-round-2025

This grant offers teachers the opportunity to pursue a PhD. The programme helps teachers combine their PhD-project with their responsibilities at school.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed

### Purpose

Teachers from primary, secondary, further vocational, higher vocational and special education can receive a grant for a PhD study. The aim is to directly apply the knowledge and research experience acquired for the benefit of educational practice. A higher number of practising teachers with a PhD also increases the quality of education and strengthens the connections between universities and schools. You can apply for a PhD grant for any research subject. However, you must be able to indicate the importance.

### Who can apply

You must hold a tenured position and you must be employed at an educational institute funded by the Dutch government.

### What to apply for

With the PhD grant a replacement teacher can be appointed for a period of 5 years for half of your contract hours, up to a maximum of 0.4 fte.

### When to apply

- The deadline for the submission of proposals is **Tuesday 9 September 2025, 14:00 CEST**.
There are two submission rounds annually for the Doctoral Grant for Teachers, with submission deadlines in January and September.

### Assessment

### Criteria

Further information about the criteria can be found in the call for proposals. The quality of the candidate and the research proposal are assessed according to three criteria:

- Quality of the research proposal
- Impact
- Quality of the candidate

### Procedure

A science-wide committee will assess the proposals and will compile a ranking. The most promising candidates will be invited for an interview. The candidate will present the research idea to the selection committee. The Domain Board Social Sciences and Humanities of NWO will take the final granting decision.

## Prepare

Applications must be submitted through the Isaac application and reporting system. If you do not have an Isaac account yet, then please create one at least one workday in advance. More information about writing a proposal and which forms you need to use can be found in Isaac. Please download and use the forms in Isaac. Below you will find examples of the required forms.

### Write proposal

Applicants are entirely free to choose the topic of their research; any research topic is eligible for consideration. The application will be assessed based on the criteria outlined in section 4.3 of the Call for Proposals. Applications may be written in either Dutch or English. In addition to the research proposal, the application form includes the applicant’s CV and a recommendation from the supervisor.

### Fill in forms

**The application consists of**:

- The application form (including a recommendation from the supervisor);
- A compulsory annex to the application: the employer’s declaration.
