# Co-creatie en Cultuurbeoefening

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/co-creatie-en-cultuurbeoefening

This funding program aims to stimulate practice-oriented research based on co-creation, with cultural practices as a central focus. The goal is to contribute to developments related to societal challenges within the themes of social cohesion or climate. Arts universities can apply for funding if they collaborate with at least one associated cultural institution and a civil society organization.
