# KIEM HighTech

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kiem-hightech

The aim of KIEM HighTech is to conduct (explorative) applied research through new practice-oriented research activities, in the field of KIA key technologies and associated roadmaps. A network of knowledge institutions and partners in the field carries out these research activities. KIEM HighTech stimulates both networking and the exploration of applied research questions, which can be a KIEM (seed) for a follow-up project or an innovation. The funding is a maximum of € 40.000 per application.
