# NGF GroenvermogenNL: Hydrogen and green electrons for nitrogen-based chemistry

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/ngf-groenvermogennl-hydrogen-and-green-electrons-for-nitrogen-based-chemistry

This call is part of the National Growth Fund programme GroenvermogenNL. The use of hydrogen and green electrons for nitrogen-based chemistry is necessary for making the Dutch chemical industry more sustainable. Researchers and companies must form a consortium to conduct research into new production methods for nitrogen-based chemicals.

### Purpose

The aim of this Call for Proposals is to implement GroenvermogenNL Work Package 5: Hydrogen and green electrons for nitrogen-based chemistry. This will make an important contribution to making the Dutch chemical industry more sustainable. A consortium will be established through workshops that will focus on innovation in making nitrogen-based chemistry more sustainable. This innovation is aimed at developing and demonstrating affordable technology for producing nitrogen-containing chemicals. These production routes use renewable hydrogen and/or green electrons.

Research areas are:

1. Thermocatalytic synthesis of ammonia(c) and nitrogen-containing chemicals.
2. Direct electrochemical, plasma-chemical and photochemical conversion to produce useful nitrogen-containing chemicals.

### Who can apply

The proposal can be submitted by a consortium existing of:

1. Researchers from universities, universities of applied and TO2 institutions.
2. Employees of businesses and other public and private organisations provided that:- They have a tenured position for at least 0.6 fte;
- Their organisation has demonstrable Research & Development (R&D) activities in the Netherlands;
- They hold a master’s degree.

### Consortium building

Consortium building takes places during two multiple-day workshops. The workshops are mandatory for the main applicant of the consortium. Co-applicants, cofinanciers and collaborating partners preferably also take part in the workshops.  Signing up for the workshop can be done via ISAAC.

### What to apply for

A maximum total of €20,317,355 can be requested from NWO for the grant application in this Call for Proposals. NWO will fund a maximum of 85% of the project, and a minimum of 51%.

You will therefore need to arrange other funding for this call, such as cash or in-kind co-funding from private parties. See chapter 3.2 of the call for proposals for more conditions.

Projects may last for a maximum of six years.

### Assessment

### Criteria

The proposal will be assessed on the basis of two criteria, both of which are weighted equally:

1. Quality of the consortium,
2. Quality of the research plan.

### Procedure

The procedure fort his Call for proposals consists of 3 steps:

1. Registration of organisations for the workshops (deadline 3 Juli 2025, 14:00 CEST),
2. Workshops (22 – 24 September/27 – 29 oktober 2025),
3. Developing grant proposal (deadline 22 januari 2026, 14:00 CET).
After submission, the proposal will be assessed by the selection committee, which will advise the NWO Domain Science Board about whether or not to award funding.

The decision of the Domain Science board is expected in the second quarter of 2026.

### Write proposal

Organisations that want to participate in the grant proposal within this Call for proposals are required to submit an Application form Workshops to participate in the procedure. For each organisation, a maximum of one Application form Workshops may be submitted. This means a maximum of one Application form Workshops for the entire organisation at the highest level (e.g. at university level and not at faculty or department level).
