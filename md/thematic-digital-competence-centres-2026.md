# Thematic Digital Competence Centres 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/thematic-digital-competence-centres-2026

This call is intended to help realise the digitalisation ambitions of the TDCCs and the communities they serve. The ambitions relate to data, software and computing. Before a proposal can be submitted at NWO, it has to complete a mandatory pre-process at the TDCC’s. Contact the relevant TDCC (LSH, NES, or SSH) before the deadline of this pre-process.

## Characteristics

**Status:** In preparation  
**Closing date full application:** 12 November 2026 14:00 hrs. (s.t.c.)  
**Budget:** €2,400,000  
**Research programme:** Digital Competence Centres
