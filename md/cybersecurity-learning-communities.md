# Cybersecurity learning communities

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/cybersecurity-learning-communities

Funding scheme for practice-oriented research activities at universities of applied sciences that stimulate collaboration between knowledge and educational institutions, government organizations, and the business community through learning communities on cybersecurity and cyber resilience.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 2 December 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Interdisciplinary Collaboration Collaboration with social partners Applied Sciences Research Collaboration with Industry Knowledge Utilisation  
**Budget:** €1,000,000  
**Research programme:** Cybersecurity Learning Communities 2025-2030
