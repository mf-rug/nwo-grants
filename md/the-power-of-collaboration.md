# The power of collaboration

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/the-power-of-collaboration

NWA stimulates collaboration between different partners, so that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts and new knowledge is developed for social challenges. In recent years, powerful collaborations (NWA consortia) have been built up within previously awarded NWA projects, which have gained knowledge about these social challenges and produced concrete perspectives for action on policy (implementation) issues.
