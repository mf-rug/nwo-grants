# Innofest X SIA Sport

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/innofest-x-sia-sport

Do you want to test a result from research that contributes to more sports, sportive exercise or making sports and sports facilities more sustainable? Then you can apply for funding with Innofest X SIA Sport In Innofest X SIA Sport you can apply for funding for such a test and for example further developing a prototype. You can apply for a maximum of €10,000.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed
