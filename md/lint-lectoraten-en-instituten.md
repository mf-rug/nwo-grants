# L.INT – Lectoraten en Instituten

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/lint-lectoraten-en-instituten

The L.INT-subsidy scheme focuses on establishing a sustainable collaboration between a university of applied sciences and a research organization around a joint research program in the shape of a L.INT-lectorate. 

The L.INT-subsidy scheme enables a lector to combine activities at a university of applied sciences with activities at a research organization, creating a sustainable cooperation that strengthens research in the innovation chain.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 30 September 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Interdisciplinary Collaboration Applied Sciences Research Collaboration with Industry  
**Budget:** €2,800,000  
**Research programme:** L.INT Lectorposities bij instituten
