# NGF-CIII Start, Exploratory research into effective and responsible IX

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/ngf-ciii-start-exploratory-research-into-effective-and-responsible-ix

With this scheme we fund short-cycle research into when and how an Immersive Experience (IX) works effectively and responsible. The aim is to convert relevant and effective practices, methods, experiences and insights into validated and generically applicable knowledge. Researchers, (semi-)government bodies, businesses and civil society organisations are invited to submit a project in co-creation with IX creators and adopters.
