# Management support NWA-routes 2025

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/management-support-nwa-routes-2025

The Route management of the 25 NWA routes can apply for funding for the development of the route and the route networks. In this Call for proposals funding can be requested for strengthening and support of the route management and the governance of the route,  broadening and maintaining the open and inclusive, knowledgechain wide, trans- and interdisciplinary character of the network and the organisation of meetings and workshops in order to involve the stakeholders in the network in the activities of the route.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 10 October 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Collaboration with Partners  
**Budget:** €2,500,000  
**Research programme:** Support NWA-routes
