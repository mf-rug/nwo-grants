# Digital product passports, application in practice

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/digital-product-passports-application-in-practice

For 2-year research on the application of digital product passports, essential to realise and accelerate the transition to a circular economy. Aimed at researchers from government-funded universities of applied sciences together with practice partners. The maximum grant to be applied for per project is € 385,000. The required minimum own contribution and co-funding together amount to at least 35% of the total project budget.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed
