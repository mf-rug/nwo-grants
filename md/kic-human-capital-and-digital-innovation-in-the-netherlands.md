# KIC Human Capital and Digital Innovation in the Netherlands

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kic-human-capital-and-digital-innovation-in-the-netherlands

The Netherlands face serious challenges when it comes to labor productivity, adaptive capacity, innovative strength and earning capacity of companies, especially within SMEs. Social and technological innovation offer promising solutions to the societal challenges of our time. AI and robotics can be introduced into business processes to work smarter, to strengthen the learning capacity of organizations and employees, and to remain relevant in the market. To promote this implementation, much more research is needed, involving actual collaboration with these technologies.

## Characteristics

**Status:** In preparation  
**Closing date full application:** 9 March 2027 14:00 hrs. (s.t.c.)  
**Budget:** €7,000,000  
**Research programme:** Knowledge and Innovation Covenant Mission 2024-2027 Human Capital en Digitale Innovatie in Nederland: het vergroten van innovatiekracht, adaptief vermogen, en verdienvermogen door AI en robotica  

## Explore

This call calls on researchers and social consortium partners (business and public stakeholders) to take an integrated look at the innovation ecosystem, the strategic HRM policy of companies and the individuals and teams in SMEs who work with AI and robotics.
