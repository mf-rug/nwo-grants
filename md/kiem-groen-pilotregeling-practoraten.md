# KIEM-groen pilotregeling practoraten

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kiem-groen-pilotregeling-practoraten

This KIEM-groen pilot call for proposals aims at research carried out by practorates at MBO institutions in the green domain.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 9 September 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**Budget:** €500,000  
**Research programme:** KIEM 2021-2025 Groene practoraten  

## Explore

### Purpose

Practorates are expertise platforms with a research component, organized as part of mbo-institutions. This call stimulates the development of (new) partnerships between practorates active in the green domain, lectorates, knowledge institutions and SME partners. This promotes the position of practorates in the knowledge chain, which helps them to professionalise further. It establishes a connection between the research infrastructure at universities of applied sciences on the one hand and professional practice on the other. The partnerships carry out joint activities in the field of (practice-oriented) research and innovation.

 

Applications relate to applicable knowledge and solving practical issues. Improving and developing one's own practice are leading. Project outcomes include network activities, prototypes, tests, (business) models, research and work methods, demonstrators, processes, product concepts and service concepts. Project results have an impact on professional practice and on the education programme.

### Who can apply

The applicant must be a government-funded MBO institution. The practor involved must be part of the CIV-koepelplan and be supervised by Stichting Ieder Mbo een Practoraat (SIMP).

At least two practical partners are involved in the research consortium, at least one of which is an SME established in the Netherlands. In addition, a research group of a university of applied sciences that is affiliated with a Center of Expertise must participate in the consortium.

### What to apply for

The maximum grant size is 40,000 per application. The practical partners in the research contribute at least 25% of the subsidy amount to the implementation of the project. This can be in cash or in kind. The duration of the subsidy period is a maximum of 12 months. The intended project must start within 3 months after the grant has been awarded.

### When to apply

- You can submit an application until September 9, 2025.

### Assessment

### Criteria

All proposals are assessed by an Assessment Committee based on five assessment criteria:

1. Fitting within the thematic delineation of the call
2. Demand articulation
3. Networking
4. Innovation within the framework of the KIEM-groen pilotregeling practoraten
5. Feasibility project plan

For further explanation, consult the call for proposals section 4.3 'Criteria' (in Dutch).

### Procedure

Regieorgaan SIA checks whether applications are eligible within 65 working days after submission. An independent assessment committee assesses each eligible application based on the assessment criteria. Each assessment criteria is scored as adequate or inadequate. An application only receives a positive assessment if it is adequate on all criteria.

The board of Regieorgaan SIA assesses the procedure followed and decides whether or not to grant a subsidy based on the assessment committee's advice. This is done on a first come, first served basis.

You will hear within 10 weeks after the date of eligibility whether your application has been granted.

## Prepare

You will find all of the documents that you need for your application here. Applications must be submitted through the ISAAC application and reporting system. If you do not have an ISAAC account yet, then please create one at least one workday in advance. More information about writing a proposal and which forms you need to use can be found below. The forms below are examples. The forms you must use for your application can be downloaded in ISAAC.

### Fill in forms

A complete application includes the following documents:

- the fully completed and signed application form;
- the project proposal;
- the budget in excel with the requested subsidy, co-financing and cost justification;
- the completed project participants form in excel with an overview of the project group members involved in the context of the NWO 'Code for dealing with personal interests'.

If you have been authorized by the Executive Board to sign the application, you must also enclose a mandate form with the application.
