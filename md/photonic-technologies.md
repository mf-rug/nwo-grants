# Photonic Technologies

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/photonic-technologies

This call is part of the R&D programme PhotonDelta, financed by the National Growth Fund. This current Call for proposals concerns NGF: Photonic Technologies that has the aim to further grow the market and supply chains in photonics in the Netherlands.

## Explore

### Purpose

The “Photonic Technologies” call is a six-year program with the aim to further grow the market and supply chains in photonics in the Netherlands. The current innovative photonic technologies that are moving from the labs to companies are based on research that has been performed in the Netherlands over the past 20-30 years. This Call for Proposals aims to fund early stage scientific research (Technology Readiness Levels between 1-4) with the aim to deliver breakthrough photonic materials, devices and systems. The program therefore focuses on photonic academic research in the fields of applied and theoretical physics, electrical engineering, chemical engineering, material and life sciences.

### Who can apply

Researchers from universities, universities of applied and TO2 institutions.

### What to apply for

For an application in this Call for proposals, a total NWO contribution of at least €400,000 and a maximum of €1,200,000 can be requested. If the total project costs are higher than €700,000, a contribution to the project costs (co-financing) by (a) user(s) is mandatory, namely 25% of the excess above €700,000. This can be in-kind, in-cash or a mix of both.

### When to apply

- Deadline proposals: **29 april 2025 om 14:00:00 CEST**

### Assessment

### Criteria

The application(s) submitted within this Call for proposals will be substantially assessed on the basis of the following criteria:

1. Scientific quality (60%)
2. Match of researcher/research team and institution(s) with proposed research (20%)
3. Contribution of proposed research to the ambition and explicit goal of the Call for proposals (20%)
