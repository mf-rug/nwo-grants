# Praktijkkennis voor Voedsel en Groen: Thematische vraagstukken, indieningsronde september 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/praktijkkennis-voor-voedsel-en-groen-thematische-vraagstukken-indieningsronde-september-2026

Universities of applied sciences can apply for funding for thematic research with the aim of promoting the contribution of universities of applied sciences in specific societal challenges in the green domain.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Open for applications  
**Closing date full application:** 29 September 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Collaboration with Industry Applied Sciences Research Interdisciplinary Collaboration  
**Budget:** €2,750,000  
**Research programme:** PVG Thematische calls 2026
