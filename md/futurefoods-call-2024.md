# FutureFoodS Call 2024

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/futurefoods-call-2024

The FutureFoodS Call 2024 aims to jointly develop an EU-wide research and innovation framework to accelerate the transition to a sustainable food system. The mission is to contribute to healthy diets that are safely and sustainably processed, consumed and governed at European level. It aims to generate knowledge that enables governments, businesses and citizens to truly enable system transformations.

## Explore

### Purpose

FutureFoods has prioritised three topics for this first call. They reflect the need for new knowledge and innovations in society and the food sector. In each application one of the following three call topics can be chosen:

**Topic 1:** The way towards sustainable and resilient food systems

**Topic 2:** New foods – Fostering innovations in food design, processing and supply via demand and supply reorientation

**Topic 3:** Empowering sustainable food choices – Enabling environments and dietary shifts

### Who can apply

The following countries will participate in this call: Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Lithuania, Norway, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Spain, Sweden, The Netherlands, Türkiye.

Research consortia must be comprised of a minimum of three partners from at least three Member States or Associated Countries participating in the call and be eligible to request  funding from the FOs participating in this call  and providing funding for the selected topic.

For applicants from Dutch universities and eligible knowledge institutions, funding is available from NWO – see Annex I of the Call for proposals for the possibilities.

The added value of transnational collaboration should be clearly stated in the application.

### What to apply for

For research proposals in this round, consortia can apply for a maximum of € 400,000 in funding from NWO.

### When to apply

- The submission deadline for pre-proposals is **15 January 2025, at 13:00 CET**.
- The submission deadline for full proposals is **9 July 2025, at 13:00 CEST**.

### Assessment

### Criteria

Full proposals will be evaluated against the following three equally weighted evaluation criteria excellence, impact and quality and efficiency of the implementation.

### Procedure

The assessment in this call will be done in two stages. In the first stage, consortia are invited to submit a pre-proposal. Only consortia that were successful in the first stage will be invited to submit a full proposal. Eligible proposals – both in the first and in the second stage – will be assessed by an independent evaluation committee.

## Prepare

Pre-applications and full applications must be submitted through the online submission tool [https://futurefoods.ptj.de](https://futurefoods.ptj.de).  Use the application forms available on the FutureFoodS website. In case of self-funded partners, a Letter of Commitment (see Annex E) should be submitted in the full proposal phase.

Applicants who apply for funding at NWO must submit a completed NWO budget form separately at NWO when submitting their full proposal. This form can be found under ‘Downloads for your application’. Please send this form to [futurefoods@nwo.nl](mailto:futurefoods@nwo.nl)

### Write proposal

A two-phase procedure will be followed for this Call for Proposals. In the first phase, consortia will be invited to submit pre-proposals. A pre-proposal includes a description of the partners, a brief project description and budget indications as described in the detailed pre-proposal template in Annex C, and in other relevant documents available in the submission platform: [https://futurefoods.ptj.de](https://futurefoods.ptj.de).  The pre-proposals that are eligible according to the transnational and national/regional criteria will be assessed by an Expert Panel, consisting of recognized international experts in relevant fields. The decision for the invitation to submit a full proposal in the second phase will be taken jointly by the participating funders and communicated to the main applicant of the transnational consortium.

### Mandatory consortium-building activities

The partners in the approved projects will be asked to sign a consortium agreement before the start of the projects. More information will be provided in the award letter.
