# KIC Data sharing for the energy transition: socio-technical challenges

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kic-data-sharing-for-the-energy-transition-socio-technical-challenges

The energy transition can proceed more effectively and efficiently with new knowledge about sharing data, with a central role for the availability, quality and privacy of the data. s NWO invites public-private consortia to collaborate on the knowledge needed to provide answers to challenges such as a growing peak electricity demand and congestion on the electricity grid.

### Purpose

The transition towards sustainable energy changes the demands on the energy grid. Generating electricity is becoming more decentralised, and demand has to follow supply rather than vice versa. The necessary control mechanisms, models, policy and investment need to have a better availability of high quality data. In order to get the right data to the right stakeholder, new socio-technical insights are required to support sharing data in the realms of privacy, business-strategy, technology and behaviour.

The aim of the call is to take away barriers that prevent the right stakeholders from having the right data in a quickly changing system. Removing these barriers should make sharing data a more structured, accessible and fair process.

### Who can apply

Proposals are submitted by the main applicant and one or more co-applicants. A proposal is written by a consortium in which besides the applicants other participants are involved as well.

The main and co-applicant(s) should conduct the proposed research as a consortium that always includes, beside themselves, two or more co-funders, and potentially one or more cooperation partners. All consortiumpartners should play an active role in the formulation of the research questions and in the design and implementation of the project.

### Consortium building

For this Call for proposals, to promote and support consortium formation a matchmaking will be used as part of the Call procedure. This event takes place on Wednesday 24 September 2025. Additionally it is possible to submit a research idea, which NWO publishes on the website and uses to improve the matchmaking process. Research ideas submitted after Tuesday 2 September 2025 14:00:00 CEST will not be used for the matchmaking process. Participation in matchmaking activities is recommended but not required.

### What to apply for

For an application in this Call for proposals, one can apply for a minimum of €1.000.000 and a maximum of €2.200.000 of NWO funding. With this, NWO finances maximally 70% of the total project budget; the rest of the funding must be contributed by the compulsory co-funding. NWO never funds less than 50% of the total project budget.

The applicant and co-applicants can include costs for personnel, equipment, investments, knowledge utilisation and project management.

### Assessment

### Criteria

The pre-proposals submitted within this Call for proposals will be substantially assessed on the basis of the following criteria:

1. Problem definition and analysis 20%
2. Envisaged impact and route to impact 20%
3. Quality of the consortium 30%
4. Quality of the research 30%

### Procedure

**The application procedure consists of the following steps:**

- submission of a research idea (optional);
- matchmaking session (optional);
- submission of the proposal;
- admissibility of the proposal;
- peer review by referees;
- rebuttal;
- pre-advice assessment committee;
- interview selection (if necessary);
- interview;
- assessment committee meeting;
- decision-making.

### Write proposal

**NWO will assess your application on the following conditions:**

- the main applicant and co-applicant(s) meet the conditions;
- the applying consortium contains two or more cofunders;
- the application complies with the DORA guidelines as described in the Call for proposals;
- the required co-funding has been, after any request for addition or change, correctly and fully committed through declarations of co-funding;
- the application is submitted via the main applicant’s ISAAC account;
- the application is received before the deadline;
- the application is written in English;
- the application budget of the proposal is drawn up in accordance with the conditions for this Call for proposals using the format made available which includes the most recent rates;
- the proposed project has a duration of at most 6 years;
- all of the required annexes and forms, after a possible request to make additions or changes, have been completed and submitted completely and according to the instructions and in accordance with the terms of this Call for proposals.
- the budget in the application has been prepared according to the terms of this Call for proposals (using the format made available which includes the most recent rates);
- the required co-financing has been correctly and fully committed through co-financing statements

### Fill in forms

For this Call for proposals we require aside from the application form a signed declaration of intent from the cofinancing parties. As potential errors in these letters have proven to be difficult to correct within the timing window for corrections, NWO offers to check these letters before the application deadline. If you wish to make use of this, please contact [kic-energydatasharing@nwo.nl](mailto:kic-energydatasharing@nwo.nl)

**On this website you find the following documents:**

- Application form: required.
- Declaration cofinancing: required for each co-financer.
- Declaration “statements and signature”: required.
- “Overzicht MMIPs en TRL niveaus”: only available in dutch, support document for filling in questions about MMIP’s and TRL level in “statements and signature”. For this call we generally recommend MMIP “2. Data science, data analytics and data spaces” in KIA Digitalisering, and MMIP “13. Systeemintegratie” in KIA Klimaat en Energie.
- Statement appointment and project supervision: in case a co-applicant does not have a permanent position, or the permanent position is foreseen to end due to e.g. retirement before the end of the project, required, otherwise no.
- Declaration research organisation: in case a foreign research organisation participates, required, otherwise no.
