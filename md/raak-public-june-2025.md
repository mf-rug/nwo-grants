# RAAK-public June 2025

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/raak-public-june-2025

Professors of applied sciences and researchers from universities of applied sciences conduct research together with professionals working in the public sector. This leads to knowledge and innovations that contribute to solving social issues.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 17 June 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €7,350,000  
**Research programme:** RAAK-publiek
