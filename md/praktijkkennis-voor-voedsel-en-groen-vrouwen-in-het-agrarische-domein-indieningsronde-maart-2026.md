# Praktijkkennis voor Voedsel en Groen: Vrouwen in het agrarische domein, indieningsronde maart 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/praktijkkennis-voor-voedsel-en-groen-vrouwen-in-het-agrarische-domein-indieningsronde-maart-2026

Universities of applied sciences can apply for funding to conduct research on what is needed to utilize the potential of women in the agricultural sector and how sector stakeholders can contribute to this.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Open for applications  
**Closing date full application:** 24 March 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Interdisciplinary Collaboration Applied Sciences Research Collaboration with Industry  
**Budget:** €350,000  
**Research programme:** PVG Duurzame bedrijfsovername 2019-2026
