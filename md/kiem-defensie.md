# KIEM Defensie

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kiem-defensie

The aim of KIEM Defensie is to link questions from Defence units to practice-oriented research activities at universities of applied sciences to facilitate (exploratory) practice-oriented research focused on Defence issues. Lecturers and researchers at universities of applied sciences may apply for up to €40,000 in funding per project. The grant ceiling for this grant round totals €840,875. A maximum of 21 applications are expected to be awarded within this round.
