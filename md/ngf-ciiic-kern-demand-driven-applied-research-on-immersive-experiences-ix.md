# NGF-CIIIC Kern: Demand-Driven Applied Research on Immersive Experiences (IX)

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/ngf-ciiic-kern-demand-driven-applied-research-on-immersive-experiences-ix

This call falls within the National Growth Fund programme Creative Industries Immersive Impact Coalition (CIIIC) and is now open for research proposals. CIIIC Kern encourages two-year demand-driven applied research into the application and deployment of immersive experiences (IX) within SMEs and/or the public sector. The Taskforce for Applied Research SIA is implementing this NGF call in collaboration with the CIIIC project organisation.

## Explore

For the section About the Call: CIIIC Kern funds projects that conduct demand-driven applied research into the application and deployment of immersive experiences (ix) within SME’s and/or the public sector. This research is carried out by knowledge institutions in collaboration with companies and/or public organisations. For the responsible and effective application and use of immersive experiences (IX) in SMEs and the public sector, new knowledge and validated methods are required. This Call for Proposals focuses on demand-driven, applied research aimed at testing and validating methods and tools for IX applications, or on the conditions and perspectives for the strategic deployment of IX.

A maximum of 300,000 euro can be requested per project. 50 percent of the research budget must be contributed by co-funding from partners.

For more information we refer you to the website of [Regieorgaan SIA](https://regieorgaan-sia.nl/financiering/ciiic-kern/)
