# Open Mind 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/open-mind-2026

Open Mind funds innovative research proposals that break through conventional thinking frameworks and use technology for sustainable social impact. Think of ideas that, for example, transform education, promote inclusivity, tackle climate issues or enable new forms of communication. The call invites researchers from all disciplines who tackle global challenges with groundbreaking and creative solutions. Researchers with exciting, high-risk, high-gain ideas are encouraged to submit their outside-the-box research proposals and compete for a chance to receive €100,000 to explore their research ideas over the course of one year.

## Explore

### Purpose

The Open Mind call aims to support out-of-the-box research ideas that offer sustainable solutions to global societal challenges through the power of technology. Whether you are pioneering new ways to transform education, build disaster-proof communication systems, empower people with disabilities, or advance environmental sustainability - we want to hear from you!

Open Mind focuses on original, creative research ideas with meaningful and lasting impact. We’re looking for high-risk high-gain ideas with the potential to be groundbreaking, either within your field or beyond. Your proposal should be bold, and challenge conventional approaches.

The Open Mind program supports one-year projects aimed at bringing these creative ideas to life, with the goal of creating meaningful and lasting impact.

### Who can apply

Researchers (including scientific personnel like postdoc and PhD with a master’s degree) from the following research institutes can apply:

- Universities located in the Kingdom of the Netherlands,
- University medical centres,
- Institutes affiliated to the Academy (Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences (Koninklijke Nederlandse Akademie van Wetenschappen (KNAW) or NWO,
- the Netherlands Cancer Institute,
- the Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics, Nijmegen,
- the Naturalis Biodiversity Centre,
- the Advanced Research Centre for NanoLithography (ARCNL),
- the Princess Maxima Centre.

### What to apply for

NWO domain Applied and Engineering Sciences has a total budget of 1.000,000 euro available. A maximum of 10 Open Mind applications will receive funding, with a maximum of 100,000 euro (inclusive of Dutch VAT) each for the period of maximum one year. The applicant and co-applicant can apply for funding for personnel and material.

### When to apply

- An (online) information session is scheduled on January 22 2026 14:00 CET.
- The deadline for submitting proposals is March 10 2026, before 14:00 CET.

### Assessment

### Criteria

The research proposal should be in line with the aim of Open Mind and contribute to solutions for global societal challenges. The applications submitted will be substantially assessed on the basis of the following criteria:

| 1. Boldness (40%): | Novelty and Innovation, Creative Vision, High-Risk High-Gain. |
| --- | --- |
| 2. Quality (30%): | Clarity, Methodological Soundness, Realistic Risk. |
| 3.  Impact (30%): | Relevance and Urgency, Gap in Existing Solutions, Meaningful Impact. |

### Procedure

NWO assigns an independent multidisciplinary Assessment Committee consisting of representatives from science and society, featuring a mix of creative minds. A maximum of 20 proposals will be selected for a pitch-interview session to be evaluated by the Assessment Committee during the annual NWO innovation festival TEKNOWLOGY on 18th June 2026, which requires applicants to be physically present. A maximum of ten Open Mind 2026 winners will be awarded funding during the closing session of TEKNOWLOGY. Applicants who receive funding are requested to present the obtained results at the following annual TEKNOWLOGY in 2027.

### Fill in forms

In order to be able to submit an application:

1. The application form must be completed.
2. A sketch should be provided regarding the research proposal.
3. A link to the video should be given in the application form.
4. The budget form must be completed.
5. In case of a temporary appointment, a Statement of appointment must be provided.
If the above is complete and you want to submit your application, please save the application form as a PDF file and upload it together with the completed Financial planning (as Excel file) in ISAAC
