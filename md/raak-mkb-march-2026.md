# RAAK-mkb march 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/raak-mkb-march-2026

The RAAK-public scheme promotes cooperation between professionals working in the public sector and universities of applied sciences. The focus of the collaboration is on knowledge development and application. Driven by the demand from professionals, networks of universities of applied sciences and professionals carry out research. The result of the research is practical knowledge for the professional practice. The research also provides a demonstrable contribution to the renewal of higher vocational education.
