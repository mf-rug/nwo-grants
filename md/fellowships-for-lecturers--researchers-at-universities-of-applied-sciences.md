# Fellowships for lecturers/researchers at universities of applied sciences

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/fellowships-for-lecturers/researchers-at-universities-of-applied-sciences

The grant contributes to the professional development of lecturers/researchers working at Universities of Applied Sciences in their roles as researchers, change agents, innovators and/or professionals. For lecturers/researchers, the programme offers the opportunity to develop and refine applied research skills. For universities of applied sciences, the fellowship offers the opportunity to strengthen research capacity and the connection between education and research.
