# NGF: Direct Use of Hydrogen, GroenvermogenNL – Work Package 3, fase 2

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/ngf-direct-use-of-hydrogen-groenvermogennl-work-package-3-fase-2

This Call for proposals aims to achieve a concrete realisation of the second phase of GroenvermogenNL Work Package 3. This Call for proposals focuses on the direct of hydrogen and hydrogen carriers (such as ammonia and LOHC) as a fuel for direct energy production and high temperature processes. As well as on use cases for industries not yet touched upon in the first phase of Work Package 3.

## Characteristics

**Status:** In preparation  
**Closing date for pre-proposals:** 2 July 2026 14:00 hrs. (s.t.c.)  
**Closing date full application:** 21 January 2027 14:00 hrs. (s.t.c.)  
**Budget:** €8,675,741  
**Research programme:** Direct Use of Hydrogen
