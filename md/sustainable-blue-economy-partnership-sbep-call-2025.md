# Sustainable Blue Economy Partnership (SBEP) – Call 2025

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/sustainable-blue-economy-partnership-sbep-call-2025

Experienced researchers can apply for funding for international research projects that contribute to finding solutions for the green, and digital transitions of economic activities related to the ocean, seas, and coasts around European sea basins. This call is titled: ‘Digitalisation and Innovation for Resilient Marine Ecosystems Businesses, and Communities to Strengthen the EU Blue Economy’s Competitiveness’.

## Explore

### Purpose

The SBEP call 2025 for proposals pools national and regional funding for the development of knowledge and innovation that boost the transition to a climate neutral, sustainable and productive Blue Economy.

This SBEP Call 2025 co-funded call aims to support transnational research and innovation addressing the five priority areas below:

1. Digital Twins of the Ocean (DTOs) at sub-basin scale;
2. Transitioning the blue economy sectors, and the development of the coexistence and the marine multi-use infrastructures;
3. Climate-Smart Planning and managing sea-uses at the regional level;
4. Blue Bioresources; sustainable fisheries and aquaculture, and new bio-based products;
5. Resilient Coastal Communities and Businesses.
Projects in this co-funded call must be impact-driven contributions to the transformation into a blue economy for a more resilient future. Applications. Applications focus on EU and adjacent waters; the Atlantic and Arctic Ocean, the Baltic Sea, the Black Sea, the Mediterranean Sea, and the North Sea.

Applications are submitted to the joint call secretariat via the European EPSS system. For more information, see: [Funding opportunities | Bluepartnership](https://bluepartnership.eu/funding-opportunities). For Dutch participation in applications, submission of national documents is mandatory; see under ‘Preparation’.

### Who can apply

Applications need to be submitted by research consortia including teams from at least three countries participating in the call.

Full, associate and assistant professors, lectors and other researchers with a comparable position may submit an application (i.e. participate in a consortium and request NWO funding) if they adhere to the regulations as stated in Annex B: NATIONAL CONTACT POINTS AND NATIONAL/REGIONAL REGULATIONS (part The Netherlands) of the call for proposals.

An application for NWO funding has one national main applicant, responsible for scientific and financial management. National co-applicants within an application for NWO funding are now also allowed. Researchers from TO2 institutions can also apply for this second call.

Applicants may be involved in a maximum of two proposals as national main applicant and/or national co-applicant (as part of a European consortium).

More information on the eligibility criteria, including the requirements from other countries, can be found in the SBEP Call 2025 Documents.

### Consortium building

To find European partners you can use the [b2match platform](https://www.b2match.com/e/sustainable-blue-economy-partnership/sign-up) between 15 September and 15 November. . This is not obligatory.

### What to apply for

A total of €2,000,000 is made available for Dutch participation in the SBEP Call 2025.

Per application, a maximum of €400,000 can be requested from NWO if there is one national main applicant, and up to €600,000 if there is both a national main applicant and a co-applicant. The application concerns a project with a maximum duration of 3 years.

There is no limit on the size of the international consortium (neither financially nor in terms of the number of partners); the national funding organisations only impose a limit on the amount that can be requested nationally by each partner.

The SBEP Call 2025 also allows for the inclusion of 'self-funded partners' in the international consortium.

Eligible costs for NWO funding can be found in the SBEP Call 2025, Annex B: NATIONAL CONTACT POINTS AND NATIONAL/REGIONAL REGULATIONS. Please note: PhD positions cannot be applied for in this call, due to the maximum project duration of 3 years. Moreover, NWO funds project-related costs. Therefore overhead costs are not eligible for NWO funding.

### Assessment

### Criteria

Eligible Pre-proposals will be reviewed on the basis of the following two criteria:

- Excellence
- Impact
Eligible Full proposals will be reviewed on the basis of the following three criteria:

- Excellence
- Impact
- Quality and efficiency of the implementation
Please see the full SBEP Call 2025 Documents for the criteria including the sub-criteria.

### Procedure

The assessment in this call will be done in two stages. In the first stage, consortia are invited to submit a pre-proposal. Only consortia that were successful in the first stage can submit a full proposal. Eligible proposals – both in the first and in the second stage – will be assessed by an independent Evaluation Committee consisting of experts from relevant scientific fields. The proposals will be ranked based on the criteria in the call text. Based on the ranking and the budget available, the national and regional funding agencies will take the funding decision. At NWO the Executive Board will take this decision.
