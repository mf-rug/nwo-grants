# Principal Investigator support

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/principal-investigator-support

The call ‘Principal Investigator (PI) support’ is aimed at supporting researchers in the Netherlands with a PI role for a space mission, experiment or instrument, to enable them to fulfil that role more effectively. The funding is also intended to enable the Dutch scientific field to benefit more from existing Dutch PI positions. This grant instrument is a joint call from NWO and the Netherlands Space Office.
