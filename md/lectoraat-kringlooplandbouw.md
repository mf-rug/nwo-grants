# Lectoraat kringlooplandbouw

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/lectoraat-kringlooplandbouw

The aim of Lectoraat kringlooplandbouw is to provide a stronger connection between universities of applied sciences and other knowledge institutions in the field of circular agriculture.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 23 September 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €400,000  
**Research programme:** PVG Lectoren kringlooplandbouw
