# Hydrogen and green electrons for speciality and fine chemicals

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/hydrogen-and-green-electrons-for-speciality-and-fine-chemicals

This call is part of the R&D programme GroenvermogenNL, financed by the National Growth Fund. This current Call for proposals concerns NGF: Hydrogen and green electrons for speciality and fine chemicals, GroenvermogenNL WP6, and focuses on the sustainable syntheses of specialities and fine chemicals.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed

**Closing date full application:** 10 July 2025 14:00 hrs.

## Explore

### Purpose

This Call for Proposals intends to come to a grant proposal for the realization of work package 6 of GroenvermogenNL: “NGF: Hydrogen and green electrons for speciality and fine chemicals”. Through workshops, a consortium will be formed which focusses on innovations to make the synthesis of specialities and fine chemicals more sustainable. This will eventually result in a more sustainable Dutch chemical industry.

### Who can apply

1. Researchers from universities, universities of applied and TO2 institutions.
2. Employees of businesses and other public and private organisations provided that:- They have a tenured position for at least 0.6 fte;
- Their organisation has demonstrable Research & Development (R&D) activities in the Netherlands;
- They hold a master’s degree.

### Consortium building

Consortium building takes places during two multiple-day workshops. The workshops are mandatory for the main applicant of the consortium. Signing up for the workshop can be done via ISAAC.

### What to apply for

In this Call for proposals, a maximum of €19.317.355 can be applied for. This amount needs to be supplemented by the businesses and civil society organisations in the consortium with ‘Funding other than that provided by NWO’.

**‘Funding other than that provided by NWO’ consists of:**

1. The own contribution, i.e. the non-fundable part of the eligible costs contributed by businesses and civil society organisations, plus;
2. Possible in cash (excl. Dutch VAT) or in-kind co-funding.
Financing can amongst others be used for personal and material costs.

### When to apply

- Deadline application workshops: **Tuesday 13 March 2025, 14:00 CET**
- Deadline proposal: **Tuesday 10 July 2025, 14:00 CET**

### Assessment

### Criteria

The assessment of the full proposal is based on satisfying the ‘Framework for State aid for research and development and innovation’ and the GBER, as well as the quality of the consortium and the research plan.

### Procedure

The procedure for this Call for proposals consists of 3 steps:

1. Registration for the workshops (13 March 2025)
2. Workshops (7-9 April 2025, 12-14 May 2025)
3. Developing grant proposal (deadline 10 July 2025)
After submission, the proposal will be assessed by the assessment committee, which will advise the board of the Domain Science about whether or not to award funding (November/December 2025).

### Write proposal

Organisations that want to participate in the grant proposal within this Call for proposals submit an Application form Workshops to participate in the workshops. For each organisation, a maximum of one Application form Workshops may be submitted. This means a maximum of one Application form Workshops for the entire organisation at the highest level (e.g. at university level and not at faculty or department level).

The main applicant submits the proposal via the NWO web application ISAAC. During the assessment process, NWO will communicate with the main applicant.
