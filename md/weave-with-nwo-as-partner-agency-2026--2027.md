# Weave with NWO as partner agency 2026/2027

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/weave-with-nwo-as-partner-agency-2026/2027

"part of Open Competition ENW-M 2026/2027"

As part of the Open Competition ENW-M 2026-2027, researchers from the Netherlands can be involved in a bilateral research proposal that is submitted to DFG, FNR or FWO. Together with partners from Germany, Luxembourg or Flanders they can submit a Weave proposal. NWO acts as partner agency when a Weave-proposal with Dutch co-applicant is submitted to the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG), Fonds National de la Recherche (FNR) or the Research Foundation – Flanders (FWO). Via this page the Dutch co-applicant submits the administrative submission to NWO.

## Characteristics

**Status:** In preparation  
**Budget:** €13,000,000
