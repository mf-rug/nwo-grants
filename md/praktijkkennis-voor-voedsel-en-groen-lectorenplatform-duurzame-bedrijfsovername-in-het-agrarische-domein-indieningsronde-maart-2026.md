# Praktijkkennis voor Voedsel en Groen: Lectorenplatform duurzame bedrijfsovername in het agrarische domein, indieningsronde maart 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/praktijkkennis-voor-voedsel-en-groen-lectorenplatform-duurzame-bedrijfsovername-in-het-agrarische-domein-indieningsronde-maart-2026

Universities of applied sciences can apply for funding to support collaboration between professors of applied research conducting research on the theme of sustainable business succession in the agricultural sector. This promotes greater strategic, substantive, and organizational alignment and knowledge sharing.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Open for applications  
**Closing date full application:** 24 March 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €150,000  
**Research programme:** PVG Duurzame bedrijfsovername 2019-2026
