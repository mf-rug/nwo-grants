# Rubicon Science 2026-2

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/rubicon-science-2026-2

The programme offers researchers who recently obtained their PhD the opportunity to gain knowledge, skills and experience by spending up to 2 years at a scientific research institute outside the Kingdom of the Netherlands. In some cases, it is also possible to apply for a research period at a research institute in the Kingdom of the Netherlands.

## Characteristics

**Status:** In preparation  
**Closing date full application:** 1 September 2026 14:00 hrs. (s.t.c.)  
**Research programme:** ENW
