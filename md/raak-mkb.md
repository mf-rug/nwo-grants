# RAAK-mkb

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/raak-mkb

Professors (lectoren) and researchers from Dutch universities of applied sciences (hogescholen) conduct practice-oriented research together with SMEs. Together they develop new knowledge and innovations that contribute to solving social issues. RAAK-mkb provides funding for these collaborative research projects.
