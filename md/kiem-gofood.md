# KIEM GoFood

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kiem-gofood

The aim of KIEM GoFood is to conduct (explorative) applied research through new practice-oriented research activities. A network of knowledge institutions and practice partners (companies) carries out these research activities. With this scheme, Taskforce for Applied Research SIA organization encourages both (sustainable) network formation and the exploration of applied research questions that can be a seed for a follow-up project or innovation. The funding is a maximum of € 40.000 per application.
