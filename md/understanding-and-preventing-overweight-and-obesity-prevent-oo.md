## Explore

### Purpose

With this call, ERA4health aims to enable scientists in different countries to build a valuable collaboration on common interdisciplinary research projects focused on overweight and obesity with a clear translational research approach.

Specifically, research projects awarded within this call will look into one of these two subtopics:

1. Understanding causes of obesity and overweight and its complications with mechanistic research
2. Prevention and public health strategies for critical transitional periods of life
The outcomes of the funded projects will contribute to reducing the burden of overweight and obesity in Europe by providing a better understanding of yet understudied mechanisms underlying overweight and obesity. **NWO solely funds projects based on subtopic 1.**

Applications are submitted to the joint call secretariat via the PT-Outline system. For Dutch participation in applications, submission of national documents is mandatory; see under ‘Preparation’.

### Who can apply

Applications need to be submitted by research consortia including teams from at least three and maximally five countries participating in the call.

Full, associate and assistant professors, lectors and other researchers with a comparable position may submit an application (i.e. participate in a consortium and request NWO funding) if they adhere to the regulations as stated in Annex I (part The Netherlands) of the PREVENT-OO call for proposals.

An application for NWO funding has one national main applicant, responsible for scientific and financial management. National co-applicants within an application for NWO funding are also allowed.

Each Principal Investigator can submit either one proposal as project coordinator or up to two proposals as simple partner. (i.e. the coordinator of a proposal cannot be partner in another proposal).

More information of the eligibility criteria, including the requirements from other countries, can be found in the PREVENT-OO Call for proposals, which can be found on this website and the ERA4Health website.

### What to apply for

A total of €2,500,000 is made available by NWO for Dutch participation PREVENT-OO. Per application, a maximum of €400,000 can be requested from NWO. The application concerns a project with a maximum duration of 3 years.

Eligible costs for NWO funding can be found in PREVENT-OO, Annex I of the call for proposals.

### When to apply

- Deadline for submitting a pre-proposal (compulsory) is **January 21st, 2026 at 12:00 CET**.
- Deadline for submitting a full proposal is **June 11th, 2026 at 12:00 CEST**.
Proposals need to be submitted through the electronic submission system PT-Outline. More information can be found in the PREVENT-OO Call Documents.

### Assessment

### Criteria

Eligible Pre-proposals and full proposals will be reviewed on the basis of the following three criteria:

- Excellence
- Impact
- Quality and efficiency of the implementation
For the criteria impact and quality and efficiency of the implementation, there are subcriteria which are only assessed for the full proposals. Please see the full PREVENT-OO Call 2026 documents for more information on the criteria including the sub-criteria.

### Procedure

The assessment in this call will be done in two stages. In the first stage, consortia are invited to submit a pre-proposal. Only consortia that were successful in the first stage can submit a full proposal. The assessment of eligible pre-proposals and full proposals will result in two separate ranking lists for topic 1 and 2. Eligible proposals – both in the first and in the second stage – will be assessed by an independent Evaluation Committee consisting of experts from relevant scientific fields. The proposals will be ranked based on the criteria in the call text. Based on the ranking and the budget available, the national and regional funding agencies will take the funding decision. At NWO the Executive Board will take this decision.

**Please note: NWO solely funds projects based on subtopic 1.**
