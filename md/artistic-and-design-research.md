# Artistic and Design research

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/artistic-and-design-research

The aim of this call is to strengthen artistic and design research methods and the contribution of these methods to applied research on societal challenges. This is achieved by means of an equal partnership between universities of applied sciences and cultural institutions from the creative industry.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 15 April 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Interdisciplinary Collaboration Collaboration with social partners Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €1,125,000  
**Research programme:** Artistiek en Ontwerpend Onderzoek 2024-2025
