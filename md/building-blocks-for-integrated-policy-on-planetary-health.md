# Building blocks for integrated policy on Planetary Health

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/building-blocks-for-integrated-policy-on-planetary-health

This call for proposals invites knowledge institutions and societal stakeholders to conduct research aimed at developing an approach that enables Dutch government policy to more comprehensively consider its impact on both human and planetary health.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 11 November 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Budget:** €2,865,000  
**Research programme:** Building blocks for integrated policy on Planetary Health
