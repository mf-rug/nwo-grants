# SPRONG ronde 2 fase 2

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/sprong-ronde-2-fase-2

The SPRONG programme supports universities of applied sciences in further building and strengthening existing research groups to grow into powerful research groups within eight years. SPRONG groups from round 2 that are 4 years along the way in their SPRONG trajectory can apply for SPRONG ronde 2 fase 2. The application can be up to € 1 million and aims to further grow the research groups into and perpetuate a ‘powerful research group’.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Open for applications  
**Closing date full application:** 22 September 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research Interdisciplinary Collaboration  
**Budget:** €14,000,000  
**Research programme:** SPRONG 2020-2029
