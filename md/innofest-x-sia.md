# Innofest X SIA

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/innofest-x-sia

Are you a researcher and want to test - with a team - a result from your research? With Innofest X SIA you can apply for funding to prepare a test at a festival, or to develop a prototype further so that it is ready to be tested at a festival. You can apply for up to €10,000 from Regieorgaan SIA. Innofest will provide a test site at a festival.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 29 January 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Interdisciplinary Collaboration Applied Sciences Research Knowledge Utilisation  
**Budget:** €40,000  
**Research programme:** Innofest X SIA
