# KIEM Logistiek

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kiem-logistiek

The aim of KIEM Logistiek is to conduct (explorative) applied research through new practice-oriented research activities connected to the challenges stated in National Knowledge Agenda Logistics. These are carried out by a network of knowledge institutions and practical partners. Taskforce for Applied Research SIA stimulates both networking and the exploration of practical questions with KIEM Logistics, which can serve as a seed for a follow-up project or innovation. The funding is a maximum of € 40.000 per application.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Continuous  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Starting Researchers Collaboration with Industry Knowledge Utilisation Interdisciplinary Collaboration  
**Budget:** €517,062  
**Research programme:** KIEM Logistiek 2024-2026
