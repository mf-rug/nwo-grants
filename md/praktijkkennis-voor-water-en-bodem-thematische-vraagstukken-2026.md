# Praktijkkennis voor Water en Bodem: Thematische Vraagstukken 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/praktijkkennis-voor-water-en-bodem-thematische-vraagstukken-2026

Universities of applied sciences can apply for funding for thematic research with the aim of promoting the contribution of universities of applied sciences in societal challenges on water and soil related topics.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Open for applications  
**Closing date full application:** 16 June 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €2,178,000  
**Research programme:** Praktijkkennis voor Water en Bodem: Thematische Vraagstukken 2025-2032  

## Explore

For more information and to submit an application, please visit the website of [Regieorgaan SIA](https://regieorgaan-sia.nl/financiering/water-en-bodem-thematische-vraagstukken/)

**Tags:** Earth sciences, Public administration & political science, Communication science, Cultural anthropology, Geography/planning, Environmental science, Water Research
