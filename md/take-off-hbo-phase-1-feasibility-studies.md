# Take-off hbo (phase 1) – Feasibility Studies

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/take-off-hbo-phase-1-feasibility-studies

The Take-off hbo (phase 1) – Feasibility Studies scheme aims to ensure that innovations resulting from research conducted by universities of applied sciences are put into practice. This is achieved by funding feasibility studies that assess the practical and commercial feasibility of the intended innovation. Universities of applied sciences can apply for a maximum of € 40,000 for this purpose.
