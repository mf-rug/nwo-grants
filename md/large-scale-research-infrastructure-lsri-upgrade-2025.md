# Large-Scale Research Infrastructure (LSRI)-Upgrade 2025

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/large-scale-research-infrastructure-lsri-upgrade-2025

Via LSRI-upgrade 2025, NWO strategically invests into upgrades of operational LSRIs related to the national Roadmap LSRI, which are embedded in the applying research organisations. By means of this, the LSRI are brought to (beyond) state-of-the-art with the aim of extending the operational lifetime of this LSRI.

Upgrading concerns investments in renewal of LSRIs and/or investments in (joint) strategic linking of LSRIs. Greening of the LSRI can be part of the investments.

## Explore

### Purpose

Via LSRI-upgrade 2025, NWO strategically invests into upgrades of operational LSRIs related to the national Roadmap LSRI, which are embedded in the applying research organisations. By means of this, the LSRI are brought to (beyond) state-of-the-art with the aim of extending the operational lifetime of this LSRI. Upgrading concerns investments in renewal of LSRIs and/or investments in (joint) strategic linking of LSRIs. Greening of the LSRI can be part of the investments. LSRIs for which upgrade investment is requested should be operational and embedded in the applying research organisations. The assessment procedure will test the extent to which this is the case.

### Who can apply

A main applicant submits the statement of intent and the proposal on behalf of the consortium. There are four possible categories of participants within a consortium:

1. Main applicant,
2. Co-applicants,
3. Co-funder(s) (not compulsory),
4. Cooperation partner(s) (not compulsory).

#### Additional conditions

Upgrade funding can only be applied for by consortia associated with LSRI for which the following

cumulative conditions apply:

- For the realisation of (part of) the LSRI, an LSRI project has been funded in a Call for proposals related to the *Roadmap 2008, 2012, 2016 or 2021*(see Annex 7);
- the LSRI is included in the LSRI landscape;
- the LSRI is long-term and future-proof embedded in the applying research organisation(s);
- the LSRI is operational (see threshold criteria in section 4.3.1).

#### Other conditions

You can find the full terms associated with this call in the call brochure on this page.

### What to apply for

A consortium can request a minimum of € 1,000,000 and maximum of € 3,500,000 (NWO-contribution) for an upgrade project with a project duration of 5 years. A contribution of at least 25% of the total project budget (NWO-contribution + contribution of the applying consortium to the upgrade project) by the applying consortium (own-contribution) is mandatory. The total budget of the proposed LSRI project amounts to minimum € 1,333,333.

Within the total investment, a distinction is made between capital investments and initial running costs. The following cost categories are possible:

- Investments,
- IT costs,
- Membership costs,
- Material costs,
- Personnel costs.

### When to apply

- The deadline for **statements of intent**and **letters of support** is **12 June 2025**, before 14:00 CEST.
- The deadline for submitting a **full proposal** is **16 September 2025**, before 14:00 CEST.

### Assessment

### Criteria

Proposals are assessed against four criteria with equal weighting:

1. Scientific importance,
2. Strategic importance and impact,
3. Embedding and organisational aspects,
4. Technical feasibility.

### Procedure

The assessment procedure consists of the following steps:

- Submission of statements of intent and letters of support, deadline 12 June 2025;
- Submission of a full proposal, deadline 16 September 2025;
- Peer review by referees, October - December 2025;
- Submission of a rebuttal, January 2026;
- First meeting Assessment Committee (interview selection), March 2026;
- Interviews & Second meeting Assessment Committee, April 2026;
- Decision by the Executive Board of NWO, May - June 2026.

### Fill in forms

#### Statement of intent & letter of support

Prior to submitting an application, the main applicant must announce the initiative digitally by submitting a statement of intent in ISAAC. The statement of intent is not assessed.

In addition to the statement of intent, a letter of support must be submitted signed by the project leader or his formal successor, from the funded LSRI project to which the upgrade is associated. Applications aimed at strategic linking of LSRIs require a letter of support per associated LSRI project.

The letter of support must be emailed to [LSRIupgrade@nwo.nl](mailto:LSRIupgrade@nwo.nl) as a PDF file (without protection) separately from the statement of intent. Other attachments are not allowed. A mandatory format for this letter of support can be found on the NWO funding page for this Call for proposals.

#### Application

For this call, we ask you to submit the following attachments in addition to filling in the application form. For all attachments, a mandatory format can be downloaded from this page or from the online application system ISAAC.

1. The completed budget format in Excel;
2. The completed and signed submission letter from the highest administrative body of the institution or organisation that acts as the official secretary of the consortium (letter of commitment);
3. For each applying institution, a signed letter of guarantee confirming the own contribution;
4. For each co-funder, a Declaration of Co-funding;
5. If necessary, statement appointment and project supervision.
