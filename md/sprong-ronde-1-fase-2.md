# SPRONG ronde 1 fase 2

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/sprong-ronde-1-fase-2

The SPRONG programme supports universities of applied sciences in further building and strengthening existing research groups to grow into powerful SPRONG groups within eight years. SPRONG groups from round 1 that are four years along the way in their SPRONG trajectory can apply for SPRONG ronde 1 fase 2. The application can be up to € 1 million and aims to further grow the research groups into and perpetuate a ‘powerful research group’.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 30 September 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Interdisciplinary Collaboration Collaboration with social partners Applied Sciences Research Collaboration with Industry  
**Budget:** €17,000,000  
**Research programme:** SPRONG 2020-2029
