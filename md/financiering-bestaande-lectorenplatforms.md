# Financiering bestaande lectorenplatforms

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/financiering-bestaande-lectorenplatforms

Regieorgaan SIA supports the further development of existing collaborations between professors of applied research conducting research within the same theme. This promotes greater strategic, substantive, and organizational alignment and knowledge sharing.

This enables universities of applied sciences to contribute more effectively to developments relevant to practice-based research, and to respond more swiftly to regional issues and national and international programs.
