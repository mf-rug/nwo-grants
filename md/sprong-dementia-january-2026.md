# SPRONG Dementia January 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/sprong-dementia-january-2026

With SPRONG Dementia, the Taskforce for Applied Research SIA wants to bring together and strengthen applied research, acquired knowledge and experience in the field of (quality of) life with dementia in a transdisciplinary SPRONG research group. A SPRONG group has the potential to develop into a powerful research group within an eight year period and consists of a collaboration between (at least) two universities of applied sciences and practice partners, such as companies from professional practice, public institutions and governments.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 13 January 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €2,000,000  
**Research programme:** SPRONG Dementie
