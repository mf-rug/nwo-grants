# Open Competition Domain Science – M 2026/2027

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/open-competition-domain-science-m-2026/2027

In the NWO Open Competition Domain Science - M, researchers can apply individually or in collaboration for non-programmed, curiosity-driven basic research. This funding instrument is meant for proposals with a research question in or overlapping the fields of earth sciences, astronomy, chemistry, computer science, life sciences, physics and mathematics.

## Characteristics

**Status:** In preparation  
**Budget:** €42,400,000
