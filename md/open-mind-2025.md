# Open Mind 2025

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/open-mind-2025

Open Mind enters a new phase with an exciting shift in focus in its 10th year milestone: impact and originality are at the forefront. The Open Mind 2025 round introduces a new call text, a striking new call image, and adjusted goals and objectives that reflect the evolving vision of Open Mind. Get ready to break the mold and drive real-world change. Open Mind is open for your bold idea with meaningful impact.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 11 March 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Budget:** €350,000  
**Research programme:** Open Mind  

## Explore

### Purpose

The Open Mind call aims to support out-of-the-box research ideas that offer sustainable solutions to global societal challenges through the power of technology. Whether you are pioneering new ways to transform education, build disaster-proof communication systems, empower people with disabilities, or advance environmental sustainability - **we want to hear from you**!

Open Mind focuses on **original, creative research ideas** with **meaningful and lasting impact**. We’re looking for high-risk high-gain ideas with the potential to be groundbreaking, either within your field or beyond. Your proposal should be bold, and challenge conventional approaches.

The Open Mind program supports one-year projects aimed at bringing these creative ideas to life, with the goal of creating meaningful and lasting impact.

### Who can apply

Researchers (including scientific personnel, PhD, postdoc) from the following research institutes can apply:

- Universities located in the Kingdom of the Netherlands
- University medical centres
- Institutes affiliated to the Academy (Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences (Koninklijke Nederlandse Akademie van Wetenschappen (KNAW) or NWO
- the Netherlands Cancer Institute
- the Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics, Nijmegen
- the Naturalis Biodiversity Centre
- the Advanced Research Centre for NanoLithography (ARCNL)
- the Princess Maxima Centre.

### What to apply for

NWO domain Applied and Engineering Sciences has a total budget of 350,000 euro available. Seven Open Mind grants will receive funding, with a maximum of 50,000 euro (inclusive of Dutch VAT) each for the period of one year. The applicant and co-applicant can apply for funding for personnel and material.

### When to apply

- An (online) information session is scheduled on **January 16 2025 13:00 CET**.
- The deadline for submitting proposals is **March 11 2025****, before 14:00****CET**.

### Assessment

### Criteria

The research proposal should be in line with the aim of Open Mind and contribute to solutions for global societal challenges. The applications submitted will be substantially assessed on the basis of the following criteria:

- Your bold research idea (60%):- Innovation and Uniqueness
- Clarity and Quality
- Realistic Risk
- Your meaningful impact (40%):- Relevance and Urgency
- Gap in Existing Solutions
- Long-term Impact

### Procedure

NWO puts together an independent multidisciplinary Assessment Committee consisting of scientists and other creative minds with unique perspectives. A maximum of 15 proposals will be selected to be evaluated by the Assessment Committee at the pitch session during the NWO innovation festival TEKNOWLOGY on 19th June 2025. Applicants should be present at this festival to pitch and discuss their proposal. During the closing session of TEKNOWLOGY funding will be awarded to a maximum of 7 proposals. Applicants who receive funding are requested to present the obtained results at the following annual TEKNOWLOGY in 2026.

## Prepare

Applications must be submitted through the Isaac application and reporting system. If you do not have an Isaac account yet, then please create one at least one workday in advance. More information about writing a proposal and which forms you need to use can be found in Isaac. Please download and use the forms in Isaac. Below you will find examples of the required forms.

### Write proposal

Project proposals (maximum 5 A4 pages with sections 2-4 maximum 2 pages) should be written in English according to the format of the application form. A sketch of the research idea should be provided together with a link to a downloadable short English-language video (maximum 2 minutes) that clearly explains the problem and the proposed solution in an inspiring way. The assessment committee should be impressed by the scientific challenge, original and innovative research approach or method and expected societal impact. Be encouraged to think outside the box and find unique ways to convey your idea, making it as compelling as it is visionary.

Audio and/or images used should be royalty-free. Please note that the video can be used for promotional purposes prior to and/or during NWO Innovation Festival TEKNOWLOGY 2025.

### Fill in forms

In order to be able to submit an application:

1. The application form must be completed.
2. A sketch should be provided regarding the research proposal.
3. A link to the video should be given in the application form.
4. The budget form must be completed.
5. In case of a temporary appointment, a Statement of appointment must be provided.
If the above is complete and you want to submit your application, please save the application form as a PDF file and upload it together with the completed Financial planning (as Excel file) in ISAAC
