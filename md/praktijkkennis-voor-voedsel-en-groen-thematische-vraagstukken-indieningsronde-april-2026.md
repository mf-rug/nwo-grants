# Praktijkkennis voor Voedsel en Groen: Thematische vraagstukken, indieningsronde april 2026

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/praktijkkennis-voor-voedsel-en-groen-thematische-vraagstukken-indieningsronde-april-2026

Universities of applied sciences can apply for funding for thematic research with the aim of promoting the contribution of universities of applied sciences in specific societal challenges in the green domain.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Open for applications  
**Closing date full application:** 7 April 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Interdisciplinary Collaboration Applied Sciences Research Collaboration with Industry  
**Budget:** €2,750,000  
**Research programme:** PVG Thematische calls 2026  

## Explore

For more information and to submit an application, please visit the website of [Regieorgaan SIA](https://regieorgaan-sia.nl/financiering/voedsel-en-groen-thematische-vraagstukken/)
