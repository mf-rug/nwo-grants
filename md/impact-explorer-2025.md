# Impact Explorer 2025

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/impact-explorer-2025

Impact Explorer is a small top-up, for ongoing research-projects in the open competition or talent line, to explore an unforeseen opportunity for impact. In collaboration with an Impact Partner.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Continuous  
**Closing date full application:** 5 February 2026 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Curiosity Driven Research  
**For specific groups:** Collaboration with social partners Knowledge Utilisation  
**Budget:** €1,000,000  
**Research programme:** Impact Explorer

### Purpose

The purpose of this Call for Proposals, identically to the earlier Impact Explorer round, is to explore opportunities for societal impact arising from curiosity-driven research. These are opportunities and ideas that were not anticipated in advance when the project plan was made, but have emerged during the lifetime of a project.
The grant is intended for activities to validate the potential societal impact of discoveries from more curiosity-driven research from the Talent Line and Open Competitions of both NWO and ZonMw and to explore a route towards it.

### Who can apply

Researchers with a running project in one of NWO or ZonMw's open competitions or talent line programmes can submit. An applicant may have one substantive submission within the Impact Explorer Call. Besides the main applicant Impact Explorer applications should be submitted with at least one Impact Partner. For specific conditions see chapter 3.1 in the call.

### What to apply for

Researchers can apply for a minimum of 20,000 and a maximum of 30,000 euro. It is not possible to use it to extend or fund appointments of temporary academic staff.

### When to apply

- Proposals can be submitted to NWO on a continuous basis from 4 February 2025 until 3 February 2026 before 14:00:00 CET.

### Assessment

### Criteria

An application will be assessed individually for:

1. Appropriateness to the objective of the programme
2. Quality and feasibility of the project plan
3. Quality of the team
See the call for details.

### Procedure

Each application is assessed individually whether it is of sufficient quality to be granted. This is done by means of a jury without referees.

### More information

The main changes of this extension from the earlier Impact Explorer round 2023 are:

- Addition of collection times for proposals in continuous submission, as described in chapter 1.3 and 4.2.3 in the call;

## Prepare

Applications must be submitted through the Isaac application and reporting system. If you do not have an Isaac account yet, then please create one at least one workday in advance. More information about writing a proposal and which forms you need to use can be found in Isaac. Please download and use the forms in Isaac. Below you will find examples of the required forms.

### Write proposal

Fill in the application form. There is relatively limited space to elaborate in details. The jury is not composed of experts from your own field, so keep this in mind when writing the application. In addition, make sure you look for an Impact Partner as early in the process as possible.

### Fill in forms

You need to complete both the application form and obtain a letter of support from the Impact Partner. In the situation that your contract ends during the project, a statement appointment and project supervision is also required.
