# KIC Digital Twinning – Actual and accurate models for insight, optimalisation, and decision-making

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kic-digital-twinning-actual-and-accurate-models-for-insight-optimalisation-and-decision-making

This Call for proposals stimulates public-private collaboration to generate knowledge for Digital Twins and to lower the barriers for their application. This occurs in a wide range of sectors to tackle societal challenges and to contribute to societal impact.

## Explore

### Purpose

This Call for Proposals focuses on the development of knowledge for Digital Twins and their application. The application of Digital Twins can enable people to better understand complex processes and gain insights for improvement, which increases productivity and promotes decision-making.

### Who can apply

This Call for proposals is open to proposals by researchers working at universities, universities of applied science, university medical center, and other knowledge institutions in the Netherlands. A consortium must contain at least one lead applicant, one co-applicant, and two cofunders, with the possible addition of one or more cooperation partners.

### Consortium building

In the period prior to the deadline for submitting the proposal, NWO facilitates a matchmaking event for this Call for proposals. Participation to this event is strongly recommended but is not compulsory to submitting an application. Upon registration for the matchmaking it is possible to submit a research idea. The matchmaking events in the KIC aim to bring together and connect researchers from different scientific disciplines and persons from societal organisations and companies.

### What to apply for

For an application in this Call for proposals one can request between € 1.500.000 and € 2.200.000 in funding from NWO. With this budget, NWO finances at most 80% of the total project budget; the remained of the budget must be incorporated through the obligatory cofounding. The applicant and co-applicants can submit for costs of personnel, materials, investment, knowledge utilization, and project management.

### Assessment

### Criteria

The full proposal is assessed on the basis of four criteria:

1. Problem definition and analysis (20%)
2. Envisaged impact and route to impact (20%)
3. Quality of the consortium (30%)
4. Quality of the research (30%)

### Procedure

After submitting a proposal it is first assessed by referees. The applicant will be given the opportunity to write a rebuttal on the reports of the referees. On the basis of the proposal, the referee reports, and the rebuttal the assessment committee selects consortia for an interview. After the interview, the assessment committee advices NWO on the ranking of the research proposal.

## Prepare

Applications must be submitted through the Isaac application and reporting system. If you do not have an Isaac account yet, then please create one at least one workday in advance. More information about writing a proposal and which forms you need to use can be found in Isaac. Please download and use the forms in Isaac. Below you will find examples of the required forms.

### Write proposal

**NWO will verify whether your application satisfies the following conditions:**

- the main applicant and co-applicant(s) meet the conditions stated in Section 3.1;
- the applying consortium contains two or more co-funders;
- the application complies with the DORA guidelines as described in Section 4.1;
- the application is submitted via the main applicant’s ISAAC account;
- the application is received before the deadline;
- the application is written in English;
- the proposed project has a duration of at most 6 years;
- all of the required annexes and forms are, after a possible request to make additions or changes, have been completed and submitted completely and according to the instructions and in accordance with the terms of this Call for proposals;
- the budget in the application has been prepared according to the terms of this Call for proposals (using the format made available which includes the most recent rates);
- the required co-financing has been correctly and fully committed through co-financing statements (see Sections 3.1.2 and 3.5.6).

### Fill in forms

In this program, obligatory annexes to a research proposal are:

- budget;
- declaration of cofunding;
- declaration knowledge security;
- a ‘statements and signature’ form.
