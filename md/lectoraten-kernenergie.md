# Lectoraten kernenergie

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/lectoraten-kernenergie

This call concerns the second funding round for the National Roadmap for Large-Scale Research Infrastructure 2021 (Roadmap LSRI 2021). Only Roadmap consortia associated to one or more of the Groups as defined in the Roadmap 2021 can apply for funding for realization of their LSRI-plans.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 13 May 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Interdisciplinary Collaboration Applied Sciences Research Collaboration with Industry  
**Budget:** €1,200,000  
**Research programme:** Lectoraten kernenergie 2024-2026
