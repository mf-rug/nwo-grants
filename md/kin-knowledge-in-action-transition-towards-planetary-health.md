# KIN Knowledge in Action: Transition towards planetary health

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/kin-knowledge-in-action-transition-towards-planetary-health

The Knowledge-in-Action subsidy mobilises science and practice for just climate transitions through doing-by-learning and learning-by-doing. Knowledge institutions and societal organisations can jointly apply for up to €50,000 for transformative, experimental intervention-projects to collaboratively accelerate the transition towards planetary health as a societal perspective for change: what possible solutions, investigative questions and transition learning objectives are needed for this?

This programme is based on the vision that the health of people, ecology and the planet should be organised in such a way that they not only remain within planetary boundaries but also strengthen ecological systems. Within this, human health, ecosystem resilience and social justice are deeply intertwined values and drivers for change. The intended outcome is interventions and/or transformative knowledge and practical breakthroughs that are actively deployed to enable the desired systemic change, so that health can truly contribute to transformative climate policy – nature-positive, regenerative and preventive.
