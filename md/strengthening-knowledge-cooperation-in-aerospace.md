# Strengthening knowledge cooperation in aerospace

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/strengthening-knowledge-cooperation-in-aerospace

‘Strengthening knowledge cooperation in the space sector’ is aimed at existing Expertise Networks that have been established under the Expertise Networks scheme around the development and use of space instruments for various substantive themes. The funding is intended to support Expertise Networks whose initial project subsidy has ended, in their continuation and the updating of their composition. This grant instrument is a joint call from NWO and the Netherlands Space Office.
