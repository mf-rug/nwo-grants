# L.INT: Lectoraten en instituten

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/lint-lectoraten-en-instituten-0

The L.INT scheme focuses on establishing sustainable cooperation between a university of applied sciences and a research organisation around a joint research programme. The research programme takes shape in an L.INT professorship. The L.INT scheme makes it possible for a professor to combine work at a university of applied sciences with work at an institute. The sustainable cooperation that this creates strengthens research in the knowledge and innovation system.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Open calls Open for applications

**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €2,000,000  
**Research programme:** L.INT Lectorposities bij instituten
