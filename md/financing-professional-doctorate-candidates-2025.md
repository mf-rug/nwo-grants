# Financing Professional Doctorate-candidates 2025

**Source:** https://www.nwo.nl/en/calls/financing-professional-doctorate-candidates-2025

Universities of applied sciences can apply for funding for PD candidates. A maximum of € 267,400 can be requested per application, of which a maximum of € 254,000 is intended for an allowance for labour costs and a maximum of € 13,400 for travel and study costs.

## Characteristics

**Status:** Closed  
**Closing date full application:** 13 November 2025 14:00 hrs.  
**Finance type:** Applied Science  
**For specific groups:** Applied Sciences Research  
**Budget:** €15,241,800  
**Research programme:** Financiering PD-kandidaten 2023-2027
